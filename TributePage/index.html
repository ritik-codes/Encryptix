Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The man who inspired a billion lives

Here's a time line of Dr. Kalam's life:

  • 1931 - Born in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 1960 - Has joined the Aeronautical Development Establishment of the Defence Research and Development Organisation as a scientist after becoming a member of the Defence Research & Development Service (DRDS).
  • 1969 - Was transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) where he was the project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III) which successfully deployed the Rohini satellite in near-earth orbit.
  • 2002 - Served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He won the 2002 presidential election with an electoral vote of 922,884.
  • 2015 - While delivering a lecture at the IIM Shillong, Kalam collapsed and died from an apparent cardiac arrest, aged 83.
